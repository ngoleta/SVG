This is my svg collage based on a fox head idea. The larger head is made out of two layers of polygon shapes. The bottom layer is the colorful triangles with a gradient applied to each individual triangle, and the top layer is a grayscale gradient. When you hover over each triangle, the opacity of the grayscale triangle you are on becomes 0, or transparent, so the color shows through. The grayscale gradient is not completely opaque to begin with so that some color shows through.

The red banner is just a line with a wide stroke in svg, and the fox head logo was created by tracing the path of a raster graphic to create a vector graphic using Inkscape. This image is also a link to a certain music video that is relevant to the fox theme.
